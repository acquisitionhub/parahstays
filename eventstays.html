The strategy behind the stays

Built for
intent, not browse

01
Proximity SEO
Venue-specific landing pages target the exact searches event travellers make — "luxury 2-bed, 2 stops from AO Arena on the Metrolink." Broad platforms like Airbnb cannot compete with this level of intent specificity.
02
Dynamic Surge Pricing
PriceLabs integration monitors event announcements and automatically adjusts rates. 2–3 night minimums prevent high-turnover single-night bookings and protect your margins around every major event.
03
Governing Body Deals
Exclusive partnerships with UK governing bodies — BJJ, Basketball, Racing — position each property as the "preferred accommodation" on official event pages, driving qualified referral traffic at zero ad spend.
Partnership opportunities

Beyond
Airbnb

We win in the UK market by building direct relationships, not relying on platforms that take 15–20% and flatten your listing into a commodity.

🏛️
Governing Bodies
Become the preferred stay for BJJ, Basketball, and Racing UK governing bodies. Members receive discount codes; you receive qualified, loyal bookings year after year.
🚇
Transport-Led SEO
In London and Manchester, describe properties by Tube stop or Metrolink station. "2 stops from the venue" converts faster than any postcode or street address.
📰
City Event Journal
A freshness-led blog updated 6 months ahead of each event. "Ultimate Guide to Staying in Glasgow for BJJ Nationals" captures top-of-funnel travellers before they ever visit a booking platform.
🎯
Tribe Marketing
Email campaigns to martial arts clubs, corporate travel managers, and racing societies. These "tribes" book in groups, return annually, and refer within their community.
